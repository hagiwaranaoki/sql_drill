  SELECT 
    a.name_kana 
  FROM 
    author AS a
UNION ALL 
  SELECT 
    u.l_name_kana, 
    u.f_name_kana 
  FROM usr AS u
ORDER BY 
  1 DESC
;

・重複を含まない場合はUNION ALLではなく、UNION
・氏名カナについて降順なので、u.l_name_kana, u.f_name_kanaをCONCATで括る

正しくは
  SELECT 
    a.name_kana 
  FROM 
    author AS a
UNION 
  SELECT 
    CONCAT(u.l_name_kana, u.f_name_kana) 
  FROM 
    usr AS u
ORDER BY 
  1 DESC
;
